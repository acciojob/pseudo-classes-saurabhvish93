First paragraph

The BSE’s 30-share Sensex lost 1,053 points, or 1.47 per cent, to close at 70,370.55. The NSE’s Nifty tanked 333 points, or 1.54 per cent, to finish at 21,238.80 on Tuesday.
Among sectors, except pharma and healthcare indices, all the major sectoral indices witnessed profit booking at higher levels but media index lost the most, shedding over 12 percent.

So far in January, foreign portfolio investors (FPIs) have pulled out Rs 16,601 crore from the domestic equity market.
